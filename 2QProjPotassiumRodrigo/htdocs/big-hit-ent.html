BIG HIT ENTERTAINMENT

Big Hit Entertainment, is a company lead by Bang Si-Hyuk. Big Hit has it's own artists Worldwide Superstars BTS and Global Super-Rookies TOMORROW X TOGETHER.

ARTISTS UNDER BIG HIT ENTERTAINMENT






BTS

BTS(방탄소년단, Bangtan Boys) is a seven member group who are composed of Kim Namjoon, Kim Seokjin, Min Yoongi, Jung Hoseok, Park Jimin, Kim Taehyung, and Jeon Jungkook. BTS debuted back in June 13, 2013 with the title track No More Dream. Currently BTS has relased about more than 120 songs since their debut. A few days Big Hit just released BTS's concept photo for their upcoming comback on November 20, 2020.





TOMORROW X TOGETHER

TOMOROW X TOGETHER (투모로우바이투게더, Tomorrow by Together) is a five member group who are composed of Choi Soobin, Choi Yeonjun, Kang Taehyun, and Kai Kamal Hueningkai. TOMORROW X TOGETHER debuted back in March 04, 2019 with the title track 어느날 머리에서 뿔이 자랐다 (CROWN).
